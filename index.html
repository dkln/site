INTRODUCTION

With over 20 years of experience, I have grown from senior full stack engineer into a tech lead — bridging the gap between engineering and stakeholders while staying close to the craft. I am passionate about building solid technical foundations — for products, teams, and architecture alike.

I believe that good architecture is an integral part of software engineering as a craft — woven into the daily work of the team, not delegated to a separate role. I focus on agile, pragmatic engineering with a heart for great UX and clean, maintainable code.
